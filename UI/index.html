About the AvianAI Project

The AvianAI project is a machine learning project that uses AI to identify different species of birds. Our team has trained a deep neural network to recognize various bird species based on their unique features, such as their size, color, beak shape, and more.

Our goal is to help bird enthusiasts and researchers easily identify different bird species in the wild. With the AvianAI app, users can simply take a photo of a bird and our AI algorithm will identify the species in seconds.

How AvianAI Works

The AvianAI algorithm is trained on a large dataset of bird images with labeled species information. We use transfer learning techniques to fine-tune a pre-trained model, enabling our algorithm to recognize bird species with high accuracy.

Our app uses the device's camera to capture an image of a bird. The image is then sent to our server where the algorithm processes the image and returns the identified bird species to the app. The whole process takes only a few seconds.
